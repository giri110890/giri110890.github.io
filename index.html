About Us

The Lean Snack was born out of the need for healthy snacking options in our country. Snacking is an important part of our diet to keep us sane between meals and especially when the late night hunger pangs hit. Most of us really do not keep track of the snacks we eat, because, well its just too much work. Our stores are filled with a myriad options, but none healthy. At Lean Snack, we decided to provide truly delicious healthy snacking options for everyone, irrespctive of the fact that you are watchin your diet, or a busy person wanting to have something on the go or just to keep our tummies happy between meals.
